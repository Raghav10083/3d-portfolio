Process

Asset Pipeline

1. Modeling in Blender

Designing optimized, low-poly geometries and models. Prioritizing shape silhouettes, clean topology, and smart UV unwrapping.

2. Texturing in Substance Painter

Baking ambient occlusion and normal maps, applying custom stylized or semi-realistic smart materials, adding custom edge wear and emissions.

3. Level Setup in Unreal Engine

Assembling modular assets in Unreal Engine, setting up materials/shaders, tweaking post-processing volumes, and placing dynamic lighting to capture the mood.
